About us

At AwaCare Medical Center, we are dedicated to providing a wide range of healthcare services designed to meet the needs of individuals and families. Our team of experienced and caring professionals is here to support you with high-quality medical care. Whether you need routine check-ups, specialized treatments, or assistance with managing chronic conditions, we are committed to ensuring your health and well-being. We take the time to listen to your concerns and work together with you to develop a personalized care plan that fits your unique needs. Your health is our priority, and we strive to create a welcoming environment where you feel comfortable and supported.

Pediatrics

Expert, compassionate healthcare for children of all ages, ensuring their physical, emotional, and developmental well-being. We offer regular check-ups, vaccinations, and treatment of childhood illnesses.

General Care

Comprehensive health assessments and personalized medical advice for adults, promoting overall wellness and early detection of potential health issues. Services include routine physical exams, preventive care, and chronic disease management.

Diagnostics

We offer advanced diagnostic testing, including blood work, imaging, and other tests to help identify and manage a wide range of conditions with precision and accuracy.

Women's Health

Specialized care tailored to women's unique health needs, including gynecological exams, prenatal care, and menopause management, all provided with a compassionate, patient-focused approach.

Mental Health

Comprehensive mental health services, including therapy, counseling, and psychiatric care, to support emotional well-being and mental health for individuals of all ages.

Physical Therapy

Rehabilitation services designed to help patients recover from injuries, surgeries, and medical conditions through personalized physical therapy programs that improve mobility and strength.
